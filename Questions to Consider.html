Sustainable Development Goals

Two of the 17 SDGs are at the heart of what this project could provide: SDG 13 (Climate Action) and SDG 15 (Life on Land). Sustainable Development Goal 13 is to “take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts”, according to the United Nations. Sustainable Development Goal 15 is to “conserve life on land”.
